About Us

An organisation dedicated to improving the quality of education by scientific approaches and experimentally proven techniques, to influence a whole new generation toward greatness.

4th Coordinate is the beginning of every life and we believe every beginning needs to be beautiful. With the vision to find permanent solutions for problems in the society through education in mind, ‘4th Coordinate Educational Trust’ was established. The trust was founded in 2013, by a group of students who have been trying to revolutionize every aspect of education and the learning process. The trust shall provide a platform for the inception of ideas, and encourage collaborative work.
