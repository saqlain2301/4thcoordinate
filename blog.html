Scholarships Provided By The Indian Goverment

The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is a digital portal which allows applicants to choose and apply from over 50 scholarships offered by the Central and state government. The portal also contains scholarships from All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the University Grants Commission (UGC), and other government bodies.
